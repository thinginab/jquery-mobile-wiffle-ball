  • You are ready for the Big Leagues!

    *** NEW IN 2.0.0 - Efficiently score Baseball and Softball too! ****

    Version 2 added an option to handle all of the complex baserunning scenarios that happen when you really are running bases in "real" baseball or softball. Big League still has all of the benefits of quick one-handed scoring, so you can still score these games one handed from a player in the dugout!

    Big League helps you easily score and track all of your real life backyard and sandlot baseball games where you use "ghost runners" or "imaginary runners" instead of running bases - in addition to scoring "real" baseball and softball. Other baseball scoring apps don't allow non-standard baseball rules and can't handle it when the batter is also on base - Big League does! Others require a dedicated scorekeeper to keep up - Big League can be scored by the batting team!

    The interface uses big, simple buttons so that people who are waiting to bat can score the current at bat easily - you don't need another person to sit and scorekeep on paper! After you are done scoring, you get sortable stats - both standard and sabermetric for both batters and pitchers. These stats can be emailed to yourself or others so that you can paste them in excel or your website - you don't have to painstakingly key in at bats to excel to get stats anymore, either!

    Big League was originally built for *Wiffle Ball, but it can be used to score all of the baseball and baseball-like games people play including: softball, stick ball, indian ball, and others.

    * WIFFLE®, the Wiffle Certification Mark and the image of the ball are registered trademarks of The Wiffle Ball, Inc. The makers of this app are not affiliated with The Wiffle Ball, Inc.

    • Short-handed scoring - not a problem if same person is on base and at bat
    • Customize the rules - score the way your league wants to
    • Big buttons, simple UI. Easy to score fast while playing (score and pass device on batting team)
    • Automatic baserunner advancement
    • Game-by-game and combined player stats. Advanced sabermetric stats.

  • Version 2.0.0

    Added all of the features necessary to deal with baseball and softball (mainly team size and advanced baserunning scenarios) and made several performance improvements.

    • team sizes configurable up to 15 players
    • configurable starting count (some softball leagues start with a 1 and 1 count etc.)
    • quick swap of home/away teams and drag reordering of batting order for quick fixes to pre-entered rosters
    • quick skip a half inning only entering the score - allows scoring only your own teams individual pitches and at bats but keeping a box score for the other team
    • fielders choice option for 'in play' balls. defaults to front baserunner out, but modifiable
    • tap baserunners to open a drag list change for any complex scenarios - extra bases in running, steals, baserunning errors, alternate fielders choices, etc.
